📋 Quick Start

  1. Sign In: Select your role on the landing page.
  2. View Map: Navigate to Map to see feeder, transformers, households, and outages.
  3. Analyze Risk: Browse Feeder and Transformers dashboards.
  4. Plan Work: Use Planner to prioritize and route maintenance.
  5. Generate Reports: Export PDFs from Reports.
  6. Consumer (if logged in as Consumer): View appliance insights and bills on My Home.
